== Installation ==

**From your WordPress dashboard**

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New',
2. Search for 'Fable Extra' and install the plugin,
3. Activate 'Fable Extra' from your Plugins page.

**From WordPress.org**

1. Download 'Fable Extra'.
2. Upload the 'fable-extra' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate 'Fable Extra' from your Plugins page.
